Exporter++ enables direct upload of identified security issues to Pwndoc or export to multiple document formats. Issues are automatically sorted by severity, confidence, and name for organized reporting.

Features

  • Export to multiple formats: Pwndoc, CSV, JSON, XML, Markdown, and Docx
  • Include full HTTP requests and responses in exported reports
  • Use custom templates for Markdown (Apache FreeMarker) or Docx (XDocReport with Apache Velocity)
  • Generate executive summaries automatically using Burp AI or provide your own
  • Export only your selected issues rather than all findings

Usage

  1. In the Target tab, select the issues you want to export
  2. Right-click and select "Extensions → Exporter++ → Send to Exporter++"
  3. Go to the Exporter++ tab and choose your preferred export format and options
  4. Click the Export button to complete the export process
